# Local Guide Context: Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India and is famous for its street food and culture.

Popular street foods in Delhi:
- Golgappe at Chandni Chowk
- Chole Bhature at Sita Ram Diwan Chand
- Parathas at Paranthe Wali Gali
- Momos in Lajpat Nagar

Local slang used in Delhi:
- "Bhai" is used casually
- "Scene kya hai?" means what is going on
- "Jugaad" means a smart workaround
